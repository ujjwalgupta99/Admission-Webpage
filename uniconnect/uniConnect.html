What is UniConnect?

“Uniconnect is an online education fair platform powered by Leverage Edu, India’s most Trusted University Admission Service. It features hundreds of universities from around the world and allows students to interact directly with admission teams plus academics from the Universities in a web-based exhibition format. Students can explore and identify their right-fit programs at the university of their choice.”
